  2. Educate and inform our Viewers:

    El-Zion Global Network TV stations have a responsibility to educate viewers about various topics, such as health, safety, and public policy. Through educational programs, documentaries, and in-depth investigative journalism.

  3. Entertain and engage the audience:

    El-Zion Global Network TV stations will always strive to entertain the audience by airing a variety of programs ranging from dramas and sitcoms to reality shows and game shows.

  4. Promote cultural exchange and diversity:

    El-Zion Global Network TV stations will play a vital role in promoting cultural diversity and fostering a sense of understanding and acceptance among different communities.

  5. Support the local community:

    Television stations often have a strong focus on their local community.

  6. Encourage creativity and talent:

    El-Zion Global Network TV stations will always provide a platform for aspiring actors, hosts, musicians, and other talented individuals to showcase their skills.   7. Promote social responsibility:

    El-Zion Global Network TV stations will positively influence public opinion and shape societal values. They will use their platform to promote social responsibility, address important social issues, and inspire positive change in the community.

  8. Foster civic engagement:

    El-Zion Global Network TV stations will encourage active civic participation by covering election campaigns, organizing debates, and providing information on voting procedures. They have a responsibility to keep the public informed about political developments and encourage citizens to exercise their democratic rights.

  9. Embrace technological advancements:

    With the rapid advancement of technology, El-Zion Global Network TV stations must adapt and integrate new technologies to give their viewers the best viewing experience. This includes streaming services, online platforms, social media, and interactive content to cater to changing viewer preferences and habits.
