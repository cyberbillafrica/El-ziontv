Meet the Convener

Nze Ikechukwu Nnamani

Akunnia Mota, King of No Kingdom

Nze Ikechukwu Nnamani, widely known as Mota and acclaimed as the "King of No Kingdom," is a benevolent philanthropist hailing from Amakpu Agbani. As the convener of the Maka ·ªå Di Mma Amakpu Non Governmental Organization, NGO, he stands at the forefront of empowering Indigenous Teachers in the community, showcasing his commitment to education and community development.

Mota's philanthropic endeavors extend far beyond the realms of education, encompassing significant sponsorships in infrastructure development and personal empowerment initiatives. His impactful contributions have left an indelible mark, transforming lives and fostering positive change in Agbani. Mota's dedication to uplifting his community reflects a profound sense of responsibility and a noble vision for a brighter future.
